Insanity with Joe

When:Fridays 7:30pm-8:30pm.
Cost:$10 (Zumba punch card can be used)

Insanity is a workout regimen similar to P90X in that it claims to improve fitness in 60 days through strenuous stamina training. Developed by Shaun T., Insanity emphasizes "max interval training", working out strenuously for 3–4 minutes and then rests or "cools down" for approximately 30 seconds before starting the process over. This is more intense than traditional interval training, during which participants alternate mild and strenuous exercise for roughly equal lengths of time. This program claims to promote noticeable results through core strength, as well as intense cardio training. Bring a sweat towel and water bottle. Room temperature water for sale $1.

Samba Conmigo with Johana

Samba and Axe are dances from Brazil, both have a lot of footwork and fast movements. This class is a high impact cardio with sexy body isolations.

All levels of dancers are welcome. Wear comfortable clothes and shoes that you can dance in or no shoes at all.
