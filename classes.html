Samba Conmigo with Johana

Samba and Axe are dances from Brazil, both have a lot of footwork and fast movements. This class is a high impact cardio with sexy body isolations.

What to expect: 1 hour of high impact dancing, 30 minutes of Brazilian Samba (Rio style) foot-work and choreography foot-work and choreography and 30 minutes of intense Axe dancing. This is a Brazilian dance very similar to Zumba, but includes more Samba movements.

All levels of dancers are welcome. Wear comfortable clothes and shoes that you can dance in or no shoes at all.

Note: The first 4 weeks of class will also be an audition for Johan's performing Brazilian Samba dance troupe.

Sessions are 8 weeks long, every Wednesday 7:00pm -8:00pm. 8 Week session = $100/person
